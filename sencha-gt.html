Our Sencha Green Tea is carefully sourced from the lush tea fields of Shizuoka, Japan. The leaves are harvested in the first flush of spring, ensuring the highest quality and most vibrant flavor. Sencha is distinguished by its unique processing method, where the leaves are first steamed to prevent oxidation, then rolled and dried into their characteristic needle shape.

When brewed, Sencha produces a bright, yellow-green liquor with a fresh, grassy aroma. The taste is refreshingly vegetal with notes of sweet umami and a slightly astringent finish. Rich in antioxidants and nutrients, Sencha is not only a delicious beverage but also a healthful addition to your daily routine.
