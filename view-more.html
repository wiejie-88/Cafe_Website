Media Coverage

National Daily Post

A rare gem among urban coffee shops, Heart & Hearth marries quality with heart. Their loyalty to fair-trade beans and sustainable practices gives their café a unique edge, not only in flavor but in philosophy. The signature espresso blend boasts rich, deep flavors, while the atmosphere blends modern aesthetics with earthy charm—a perfect spot for coffee lovers and casual visitors alike.

Featured in the lifestyle section of National Daily Post for its “Commitment to Community and Craftsmanship.”




Gourmet Traveler Magazine

Heart & Hearth stands out with its passion for quality and authenticity. From the moment you step in, you’re enveloped by the rich aroma of freshly roasted beans and an ambiance that feels both cozy and refined. The baristas craft each cup with an artistry that makes this café a true haven for coffee aficionados. Their house blend is complex, with notes of dark chocolate and a hint of citrus—a testament to their meticulous sourcing and roasting process.

“Top 10 Must Visit Cafés” - Gourmet Traveler Magazine, noted for its sustainable dedication to premium quality.

Caffeine Connoisseur

The hype is real! Just visited Heart & Hearth, and it’s hands down one of the best cafés I’ve been to this year. Their pour-over is absolute perfection—so smooth, with this amazing hint of caramel. Plus, the place has such a welcoming vibe and the team knows their craft inside out. Highly recommend for true coffee lovers looking for that perfect brew experience! #CoffeeLovers #Heart & Hearth”

Instagram Post by Caffeine Connoisseur (Coffee Blogger with 120k followers)





Malaysia Foodie

I had heard so much about Heart & Hearth and finally made it down to see what all the fuss was about. Safe to say, it exceeded expectations! Not only is the coffee here incredible—their signature lattes are smooth and flavorful—but the entire experience feels personal. The baristas are friendly and knowledgeable, always ready to share stories about the beans and how each roast differs. This is a coffee shop with heart!
