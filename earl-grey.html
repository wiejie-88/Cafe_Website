Our Earl Grey is a refined blend of high-quality black tea leaves infused with the essence of bergamot, a citrus fruit native to Italy. This aromatic combination results in a tea with a bright flavor and a distinctive floral aroma that has made it a beloved classic for centuries.

The black tea base provides a robust foundation, while the bergamot oil adds a layer of complexity with its citrusy and slightly floral notes. This balance creates a tea that is both invigorating and soothing, suitable for morning pick-me-ups or afternoon refreshment.
