Our Jasmine Pearls are a true testament to the art of tea crafting. Young, tender green tea leaves are carefully hand-rolled into small pearls and then naturally scented with fresh jasmine blossoms over several nights. This process infuses the tea with the delicate, floral essence of jasmine without overpowering the smooth character of the green tea.

When steeped, these pearls gracefully unfurl, releasing a captivating aroma and a sweet, rounded flavor that balances the green tea's light astringency with the soft, fragrant notes of jasmine. The result is a luxurious cup that can be enjoyed throughout the day and is particularly refreshing when served over ice.
