Our English Breakfast tea is a classic blend that has been perfected over generations. We combine premium Assam teas from India, known for their malty flavor and full body, with Ceylon teas from Sri Lanka, which add a bright, crisp character to the mix. The result is a well-balanced, full-bodied tea that's perfect for starting your day.

This robust blend stands up well to milk and sugar, making it the ideal companion for a traditional English breakfast. However, it's equally enjoyable on its own, revealing complex flavors and a satisfying depth. Whether you're an early riser or just in need of a pick-me-up, our English Breakfast tea delivers a bold, invigorating cup every time.
