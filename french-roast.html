Our French Roast represents the pinnacle of dark roasting expertise. These carefully selected beans are roasted to perfection, developing intense smoky flavors and a glossy, oily surface that's characteristic of this classic roasting style.

The result is a bold, full-bodied coffee with pronounced bittersweet notes of dark chocolate and a caramelized sweetness. The extended roasting process reduces acidity while creating a rich, intense flavor that stands up beautifully to milk or can be enjoyed black for a robust coffee experience.
