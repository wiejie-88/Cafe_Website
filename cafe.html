What makes our
Coffee Special?

The Beginning

Our journey starts with sourcing high-quality coffee seeds from trusted, sustainable farms. We prioritize quality from the very start to ensure each bean meets our standards.

Harvesting

During harvesting, only the ripest coffee cherries are handpicked by experienced farmers, ensuring optimal flavor and richness. This meticulous selection process sets the foundation for great coffee.

Post Harvesting

After harvesting, the beans go through a drying and processing stage to remove moisture and enhance their natural flavors, preparing them for the next steps in the journey.

Green Bean Selection

Each green coffee bean undergoes a rigorous inspection to ensure it meets our quality and sustainability standards. Only the finest beans make it through to the next step.

First Cupping

Our expert cuppers taste the beans for quality, evaluating flavor, aroma, and body. This critical step helps us identify the unique characteristics of each batch.

Shipping

Once selected, our beans are carefully packed and shipped to ensure they reach you at peak freshness, ready to be enjoyed in every cup of coffee.
